Lisa Dem

Lisbon based DJ in Deep Techno

Soundcloud

Born in Siberia, Lisa was growing up involved with music constantly, beginning from her parent's authentic and diverse musical influence, later she's been playing violin around 16 years in total, first in the music college orchestra and after - in a folk rock band Veter Oseni, until she moved to Europe and got passionate about electronic music, raving and DJing, starting mixing Deep House, but then moved to Techno. In her mixes she raises energy combining tracks of various techno styles, such as Hypnotic, Ambient Techno and Break beats.
